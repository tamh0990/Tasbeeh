## Packages
framer-motion | Smooth animations for the tap button and page transitions
next-themes | Dark/Light mode management
date-fns | Date formatting for statistics
recharts | Visual charts for the statistics screen

## Notes
- `navigator.vibrate` is used for haptic feedback as requested, though iOS Safari support may vary depending on the OS version.
- Entire app is wrapped in `dir="rtl"` to support Arabic.
- Uses `Tajawal` font from Google Fonts for a modern, clean Arabic typography.
